Who We Are

Based in the heart of the Eastern Cape, Amatola Roof & Floor Restorers was founded on a simple premise: professional craftsmanship should be personal. We've spent years restoring the structural integrity and aesthetic beauty of homes across the region.

Our reputation is built on honesty, reliable workmanship, and a deep understanding of local environmental factors that affect your home's exterior and interior surfaces. We don't just fix problems; we provide lasting restoration solutions that protect your most valuable investment.
